Nostos Erudire — your study — is a place to allow you to manage your eruditely matters such as the books which you wish to read, and the tasks which you must perform.

Here, you can find the Tasks and Library rooms. You may manage your lists of tasks and books-to-read using them; adding items, removing them and also marking items as 'Done'. You can also add any details or references (in the form of links) for each of your items.

The "Explore" tab to the right can lead you to these rooms.
