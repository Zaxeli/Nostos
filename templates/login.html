Welcome to Nostos

Hello, Nostos is a place where you can easily manage your various interests and hobbies. This web application provides you with different lists for things like reading, playing games, watching movies and even your tasks, all provided in a single convenient place without the hassle of maintaining each of these lists separately.

You might have even run into situations where you would have had to maintain even a single list in multiple places because of different platforms such as for games or movies. With Nostos you can maintain all of them in a single place.
